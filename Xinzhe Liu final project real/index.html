So…What about Jorge Luis Borges?

Jorge Luis Borges 24 August 1899 – 14 June 1986 was an Argentine short-story writer, essayist, poet and translator, and a key figure in Spanish-language and universal literature.

Jorge Francisco Isidoro Luis Borges Acevedo was born into an educated middle-class family on 24 August 1899. Jorge Luis Borges was taught at home until the age of 11, was bilingual in Spanish and English, reading Shakespeare in the latter at the age of twelve. The family lived in a large house with an English library of over one thousand volumes;

Death:
He died of liver cancer on 14 June 1986, aged 86, in Geneva. His burial was preceded by an ecumenical service at the Protestant Cathédrale de Saint Pierre on 18 June. Borges was a man who had unceasingly searched for the right word, the term that could sum up the whole, the final meaning of things.
